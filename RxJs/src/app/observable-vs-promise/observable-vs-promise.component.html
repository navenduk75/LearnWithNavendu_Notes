Observable vs Promise

Both are used to handle asynchronous operations. But Observables are more powerful and flexible than promises. It has more features than promises.

Promises are simpler and well-suited for situations where a single asynchronous result is expected.

Consider the specific requirements of your application and choose the asynchronous pattern that best fits your needs.
